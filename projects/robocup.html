Current Work

As Controls Lead for the WolverBot Kickers at the University of Michigan, I'm leading the multidisciplinary development of hybrid humanoid robot control systems for 18-DOF bipedal soccer-playing robots. This project combines traditional feedback control with reinforcement learning to achieve stable, dynamic locomotion.
